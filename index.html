The RigorDIB Advantage

Why Dry Ice Blasting?

Traditional cleaning methods leave residue, use harsh chemicals, or require costly disassembly. Dry ice blasting does none of that.

No Secondary Waste

CO₂ sublimates on contact, leaving only the removed contaminant behind — no water, no solvent, no sand to clean up.

Non-Abrasive

Safe for sensitive surfaces, electronics, and precision tooling. Cleans without etching, scratching, or dimensional change.

No Water Damage

Ideal for electrical equipment and moisture-sensitive environments where wet cleaning is simply not an option.

Faster Turnaround

Clean in place without disassembly. Get equipment back online faster with less labor and less downtime.

The Process

How Dry Ice Blasting Works

Four steps, one clean result.

Pellets Loaded

Solid CO₂ dry ice pellets are loaded into the blasting unit and accelerated through a specialized nozzle.

Kinetic Impact

Pellets strike the surface at high velocity, creating micro-thermal shock that breaks the bond between the contaminant and substrate.

Instant Sublimation

Upon impact, the dry ice converts directly to CO₂ gas — lifting and carrying contaminants away from the surface.

Clean Surface

The result is a clean, dry surface with no residue, no moisture, and no damage to the substrate.
