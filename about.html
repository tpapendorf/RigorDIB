Our Story

Cleaning Built Around Your Operation

Rigor Dry Ice Blasting was founded on a simple premise: there's a better way to clean industrial and commercial environments. Traditional methods — pressure washing, chemical solvents, sandblasting — create secondary waste, introduce moisture, and often require costly disassembly and reassembly of equipment.

Dry ice blasting solves all of that. CO₂ pellets strike the surface, sublimate on contact, and lift contamination away without leaving a trace. No water. No chemicals. No abrasive media. The result is faster cleaning, less downtime, and a safer process for both equipment and personnel.

We bring that technology to manufacturing floors, restoration job sites, and electrical facilities — backed by rigorous preparation, professionalism, and a commitment to results our clients can see.
