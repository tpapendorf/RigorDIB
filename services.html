Our Services

Precision dry ice blasting for three of the most demanding cleaning environments in industry and commercial restoration.

Industrial Cleaning

Automotive & Manufacturing

Production lines, tooling, and paint booths accumulate grease, release agents, coatings, and carbon buildup that standard cleaning can't touch without costly downtime. Dry ice blasting cleans in place, without disassembly, without chemicals, and without moisture.

  • Paint booth walls, fixtures, and conveyors
  • Injection molds and release agent removal
  • Welding fixtures and robotic tooling
  • Production equipment and conveyor systems
  • Carbon and grease buildup on machinery
  • Pre-paint surface preparation

Restoration Cleaning

Mold & Fire/Smoke Remediation

Mold, char, and smoke residue penetrate porous surfaces and are notoriously difficult to fully eliminate. Dry ice blasting removes contamination at the surface level without introducing moisture, protecting structural materials and accelerating the restoration timeline.

  • Structural mold removal from timber, OSB, and concrete
  • Fire char and soot removal from framing and surfaces
  • Smoke odor source elimination
  • Historic and heritage structure restoration
  • Insurance-ready documentation support
  • Safe for occupied structures when properly staged

Electrical Cleaning

Electrical & Utilities

Dust, carbon tracking, oil, and contamination on electrical equipment are fire hazards and efficiency killers. Dry ice blasting is non-conductive, non-abrasive, and leaves no residue — making it the safest and most thorough way to restore electrical equipment to spec.

  • Switchgear and distribution panels
  • Generators and electric motors
  • Transformers and substations
  • Control panels and relay cabinets
  • Bus ducts and electrical insulators
  • Carbon tracking and arc flash residue removal
